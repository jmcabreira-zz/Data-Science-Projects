In this video, titled as "Study plan for data science" , the detailed study plan is provided so that people can learn data science from scratch on their own. The relevant resources are also provided in the video.

Below topics are covered in this video
1. Identifying your challenge in study of data science
2. How to learn python coding and its important topics
3. How to learn statistics and mathematics with important topics
4. Topics for next video
