Join me doing a whole body bodyweight workout while my machine learning model trains.

If you've ever trained a machine learning model, you know you've got to fill the time without something and I can't think of anything better to get moving.

Day 11 #repsforrona (back to basics):
- Pushups
- Kicksits
- Mountain climbers
- Lunges
- Squats
- 30 seconds on, 30 seconds off, 4 times (20-minutes total)

Stay alert, stay safe, keep moving.
