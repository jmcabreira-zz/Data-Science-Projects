Published on Feb 14, 2020

Trupti started her career with L&T, after doing her engineering in electronics. In her 3.5 years of working there, she only had the opportunity to work with the basics of Python.

She found that she enjoyed working with Python, so she switched to Cap Gemini, where she was able to work on Python projects. However, she was working exclusively on automation testing projects, and she found it monotonous.

Trupti then decided to learn advanced Python, in order to do something different, and that is how she found Data Science.

She found out about GreyAtom online. Even though there were several cheaper alternatives with 100% placement guarantees, she felt that the counsellor at GreyAtom was honest. She was told that there would be no placement guarantee, but this was not the course to join for a job. She was convinced when the counsellor said that she should join only if she wanted learn Data Science.

The GreyAtom counsellor went on to say that the program would prepare her for a job, so that she wouldnâ€™t need GreyAtom to get a job at all.

Trupti used GLabs extensively, noting that the leaderboard showed points for every assignment and task that was completed. She constantly felt motivated to be on the top.

She found the mock interviews part of career services very useful, as industry experts came in and interviewed learners. Later on, they provided feedback on the weaker points of each learner, asking them to focus on those. Additionally, there were individual sessions, where resumes were reviewed.

Trupti accomplished her goal of changing her career, completing the course on time, and left GreyAtom with 2 concrete offers in hand.
