Published on Mar 26, 2020

t-SNE is an algorithm for dimensionality reduction that is well-suited to visualize high-dimensional data. The name stands for t-distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding. The idea is to embed high-dimensional points in low dimensions in a way that respects similarities between points. Nearby points in the high-dimensional space correspond to nearby embedded low-dimensional points, and distant points in high-dimensional space correspond to distant embedded low-dimensional points. #MachineLearning #tSNE
