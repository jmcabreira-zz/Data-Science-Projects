In support of IMS Pro school (http://bit.ly/ds-ims) and PyData community (http://bit.ly/pydata-pune), we are organizing a live Q&A with working Data Science professionals.

*) Your experience in Data Science?
*) What tools are used in the industry?

*) Some interview questions may ask by recruiters?
