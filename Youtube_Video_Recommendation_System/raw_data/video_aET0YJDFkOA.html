Published on Feb 13, 2020

Richard Potter is the CEO of Peak, an artificial intelligence company that drives businesses forward by giving them the tools to optimise their supply chains, streamline how they use their data and select which part of their businesses to focus on. He was formerly at UBS Investment Bank, Wolfson Microelectronics and Cooper Software, and has been at Peak since 2014.

In this video, Richard explains how artificial intelligence can be used by companies to help them become more profitable and sustainable at the same time. He explains how big companies are already starting to make every element of their business more sustainable, and how artificial intelligence can help companies harness hyperpersonalisation and new technologies to make sure their practices can save money â€“ and the planet.
