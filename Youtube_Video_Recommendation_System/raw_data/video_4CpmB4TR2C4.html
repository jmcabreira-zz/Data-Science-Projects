Published on Apr 12, 2019

In this video I bring you with me on a day that I work from home. I show you my morning routine and the nature of my work.

Unfortunately, this day was not overwhelmingly exciting. Many people believe data science to be an extremely sexy job; however, most of the time, it is data manipulation and quality analysis.

Data Science is extremely sedentary, so I mix in exercise to find some balance.
