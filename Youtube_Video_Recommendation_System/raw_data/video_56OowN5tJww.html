Published on Mar 20, 2015

This deploying printers with Active Directory video, covers three methods of deploying printers with AD. It follows along in the 70-410 series and the ExamRef book for objective 2.2. In this video I will cover deploying printers with GPO via the Print Management console. We will further investigate the problems associated with deploying a printer using the Print Management console. We will also cover deployment of printers via GPO and preferences. I further cover item level targeting which overcomes some problems. We also examine how to setup and use location based printing also known as printer location tracking with Active Directory. I explain how to setup the location schema in Active Directory Sites and Services for Sites and Subnets as well as how printer location tracking works and what the user will expect to see.
